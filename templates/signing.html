About Advanced Electronic Signatures

An Advanced Electronic Signature (AES) is a digital signature that complies with Section 13 of the Electronic Communications and Transactions Act 25 of 2002 (ECTA). It provides the same legal effect as a handwritten signature and ensures document integrity.
